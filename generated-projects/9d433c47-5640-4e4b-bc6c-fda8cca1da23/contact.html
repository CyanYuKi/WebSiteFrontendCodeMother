Frequently Asked

What is the typical turnaround time?

Portrait & personal projects: 7-10 business days. Commercial & editorial campaigns: 14-21 days depending on post-production scope and final asset delivery requirements.

Do you provide retouched files?

Yes. All delivered images include professional color grading and standard retouching. Advanced manipulation, compositing, or print-specific prep is available at a separate creative fee.

How does commercial licensing work?

Usage rights are tailored per project. Basic editorial/social media use is included. Advertising, print, merchandise, or multi-platform distribution requires a separate licensing agreement based on scope.

Do you travel for shoots?

Globally available. I regularly work across Europe, North America, and Asia. Travel, accommodation, per diems, and equipment transport are quoted separately based on destination and shoot duration.
